Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

ZigZag Rush respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use information in the app.

1. Information Collected

The app may collect:

  • Gameplay data (score, progress, session statistics) stored locally.
  • Advertising data for ad delivery and performance.
  • Device identifiers used by Google AdMob to serve ads.

2. Ads and Consent

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. If you are in the European Union or United Kingdom, we request consent before personalized ads are shown.

You can choose:

  • Personalized ads
  • Non-personalized ads
  • Decline ads

3. Data Usage

We use data to:

  • Improve the game experience
  • Serve appropriate ads based on consent
  • Analyze app performance

4. Local Storage

Most game data is stored locally on your device with localStorage. No game progress is uploaded unless you explicitly use online features.

5. External Links

Clicking external links in this policy will open your device browser.
